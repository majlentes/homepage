Life outside of the work

Besides work, my main focus is directed to my passion. Since 2010 I'm learning variety of dance styles: locking, popping, house, hip-hop, authentic jazz, contemporary, and few more. It helped me a lot to improve my focus and ability to learning. I started to be much more cofident and open. Dance is powerfull tool to increase creativity and social skills, so I highly reccomend it to every person working in programming fields!

Street Dance events around the world

Very important part of dancers life is travelling. Going abroad to connect with strangers through passion is priceless experience. More you travel more countries start to feel like your second home and people there like your extended family. So far I've already visited Sweden, Germany, Spain, Italy, France, Lithuania, Czech Republic, Slovakia and Russia. Because of this lifestyle I've started to feel I need a change of my career. I don't want to feel stuck in one place, there is much more to discover. On the video under, you can see recap from one of the events I've participated in. I inviteyou to watch it and feel the vibe!

Shows with NBDS Team crew

Most of the commercial work I'm getting involved are prepared with a dance crew/company in Lublin, Poland. NBDS Team is shortcut from the name of the New Boogie Down School. We started together as a representative team of dancers from the school mentioned before but now we treat each other more like a family you choose. In 2016 we participated in Polish Got Talent and made it to the final.
